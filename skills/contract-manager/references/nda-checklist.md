# NDA Checklist

> **Tier System**
>
> - **CRITICAL**: Must be present. Missing critical items BLOCK document generation.
> - **IMPORTANT**: Should be present. Missing items generate WARNINGS in the review.
> - **OPTIONAL**: Nice to have. Missing items generate SUGGESTIONS.

---

## CRITICAL

- [ ] **Definition of Confidential Information** — Clear, specific definition of what constitutes confidential information, including categories (technical, business, financial)
- [ ] **Receiving Party Obligations** — Specific duties: non-disclosure, non-use, limited access, standard of care (at least "reasonable care")
- [ ] **Term and Duration** — Agreement effective date, confidentiality period, and survival period after termination
- [ ] **Permitted Disclosures** — Exceptions: court order, regulatory requirement, prior written consent, need-to-know employees/advisors (who must be bound)
- [ ] **Remedies for Breach** — Acknowledgment that breach causes irreparable harm, right to seek injunctive relief, not limited to monetary damages
- [ ] **Parties Identification** — Full legal names, entity types, addresses, and authorized signatories for all parties
- [ ] **Return/Destruction of Materials** — Obligation to return or destroy all confidential materials upon termination or request, with certification requirement
- [ ] **DTSA Whistleblower Notice** — Federal Defend Trade Secrets Act immunity notice (required for US contracts since 2016)

## IMPORTANT

- [ ] **Exclusions from Confidential Information** — Publicly available, independently developed, previously known, received from third party without restriction
- [ ] **Non-solicitation of Employees** — Restriction on hiring/soliciting the other party's employees during term + specified period
- [ ] **Governing Law and Jurisdiction** — Which state/country law applies, venue for disputes
- [ ] **Assignment Restrictions** — Whether the agreement can be assigned, and under what conditions
- [ ] **Residual Knowledge** — Whether receiving party can use general knowledge/skills retained in unaided memory
- [ ] **Marking Requirements** — Whether confidential information must be marked/labeled as such
- [ ] **Mutual vs. Unilateral** — Clear statement of whether obligations flow one-way or both ways
- [ ] **Notice Requirements** — How and where to send notices, required delivery method, deemed receipt timing

## OPTIONAL

- [ ] **Non-compete Provisions** — Restrictions on competitive activities during/after term
- [ ] **Standstill Provisions** — Restrictions on acquisition attempts during NDA period
- [ ] **Export Control Compliance** — Acknowledgment of export restrictions on technical data
- [ ] **Data Protection/GDPR** — If confidential info includes personal data, compliance provisions
- [ ] **Insurance Requirements** — Whether parties must maintain specific insurance coverage

# Website Extraction Format

Use this template when producing the extraction document. Fill in all sections; mark any section as "Not found" only if genuinely absent after checking.

---

```markdown
# Site Extraction: [Site Name]

**URL**: [full URL]
**Extracted**: [date]
**Pages extracted**: [list of pages, or "Single page"]

---

## Site Overview

- **Purpose**: [What the product/service does in 1-2 sentences]
- **Target audience**: [Who this is for]
- **Core value proposition**: [The #1 claim or promise the site makes]
- **Stage/context**: [e.g., "Early-stage SaaS", "Established enterprise product", "Agency"]

---

## Brand Identity

### Colors

List every color used, with its role. Use hex values extracted from code.

| Role | Hex | Where used |
|------|-----|------------|
| Primary | #... | CTA buttons, key headings |
| Secondary | #... | Accents, highlights |
| Background | #... | Page background |
| Surface | #... | Cards, panels |
| Text primary | #... | Body copy |
| Text muted | #... | Captions, secondary labels |
| Border | #... | Dividers, card borders |

### Typography

- **Heading font**: [Name + weight, e.g. "Inter 700" or "Cal Sans"]
- **Body font**: [Name + weight]
- **Monospace** (if used): [Name]
- **Font source**: [Google Fonts / self-hosted / system]

### Visual Style

[2-3 sentences: Is it minimal or dense? Dark or light? Photography or illustration? Geometric or organic? Rounded or sharp? What's the overall aesthetic feel?]

### Tone & Voice

[2-3 sentences: Formal or casual? Technical or accessible? Bold and direct or soft and nurturing? What kind of reader does the copy assume?]

---

- [Item label] → [URL or anchor]
- CTA button: "[Label]" → [URL]

### Footer Nav (if different)

- [Column heading]: [Item 1], [Item 2], ...
```
